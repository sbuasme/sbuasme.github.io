Internship Panel
Wednesday, 11/06/2022
6:00PM - 8:00PM
Student Activities Center 305
Join ASME in collaboration with other CEAS Clubs tomorrow for an opportunity to search for and apply to Summer 2023 internships with the help of our Career Coach and student leaders! This is an interactive workshop, with a presentation from the IT/Engineering Career Coach and a panel discussion from students in CEAS, followed by a Workathon. Topics will include personal branding, seeking out mentorship, and advocating for yourself as an intern in the workplace. Pizza will be served!

Go Baby Go
Monday, 12/05/2022
7:00PM - 8:00PM
Light Engineering 173
Go Baby Go is a program that provides modified, ride-on cars to children with cerebral palsy and other disabilities. We will make modifications to a ride-on car including a push button attached to the steering wheel, extra seating support, and more. These additions will make it possible for children with muscular limitations to gain some independence as they ride around on their own!
